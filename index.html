Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our financial consulting services

What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with a diverse range of businesses including new startups, existing SMEs, mid-market companies, and businesses in special situations requiring financial restructuring. Our expertise spans across various industries and business stages.
How long does the consultation process take?
The initial discovery consultation typically takes 1-2 weeks. The overall timeline depends on the complexity of your requirements and the scope of services needed. We provide clear timelines during our initial engagement.
What is your fee structure?
Our fee structure is tailored to each engagement and can be based on project scope, retainer basis, or success-linked arrangements. We discuss and agree on fees transparently during our initial discussions.
Do you assist with international funding?
Yes, we have extensive experience in facilitating international funding and can help businesses explore cross-border financing options, including foreign direct investment, external commercial borrowings, and strategic partnerships.
Can you help with stressed asset resolution?
Absolutely. We specialize in stress resolution services and work closely with legal partners to help businesses navigate corporate insolvency, financial restructuring, and turnaround strategies to restore financial health.
What makes Accelera Consultants different?
Our founders bring 40+ years of combined experience from leading banks and financial institutions. We offer a unique blend of banking expertise, CA knowledge, and practical business understanding to deliver comprehensive, results-driven financial solutions.
